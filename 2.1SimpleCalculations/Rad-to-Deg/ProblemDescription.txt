10. Radians to Degrees 

Write a program, that reads an angle in radians (rad) and converts it to degrees (deg). 
Look for a proper formula on the Internet. 
The number π in C# programs is available through Math.PI. 
Round the result to the nearest integer using the Math.Round(…) method.

Input	  Output
3.1416	180
6.2832	360
0.7854	45
0.5236	30
