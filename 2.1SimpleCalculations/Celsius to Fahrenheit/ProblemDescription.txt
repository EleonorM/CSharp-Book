09. Celsius to Fahrenheit 

Write a program that reads degrees on Celsius scale (°C) and converts them to degrees on Fahrenheit scale (°F).
Look on the Internet for a proper formula to do the calculations.
Round the result to 2 digits after the decimal point. Here are a few examples:

Input	  Output
25	    77
0	      32
-5.5	  22.1
32.3	  90.14
