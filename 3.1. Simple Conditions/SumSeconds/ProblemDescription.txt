07. Sum Seconds 

Three athletes finish in a particular number of seconds (between 1 and 50). 
Write a program that introduces the times of the contestants and calculates their total time in "minutes:seconds" format. 
Seconds need to be zeroed at the front (2 -> "02", 7 -> "07", 35 -> "35").

Input	  Output
35
45
44	    2:04

22
7
34	    1:03

50
50
49	    2:29

14
12
10	    0:36
