14. Time + 15 Minutes 

Write a program that inputs hours and minutes of a 24-hour day and calculates what will be the time after 15 minutes. 
Print the result in hh:mm format. Hours are always between 0 and 23, and minutes are always between 0 and 59. 
Hours are written with one or two digits. Minutes are always written with two digits and zero at the front when needed.

Input	  Output
1
46	    2:01

0
01	    0:16

23
59	    0:14

11
08	    11:23

12
49	    13:04
