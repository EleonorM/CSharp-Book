11. Cinema 

In a cinema hall the chairs are ordered in a rectangle shape in r rows and c columns. 
There are three types of screenings with tickets of different prices:

Premiere – a premiere screening, with price 12.00 EUR.
Normal – a standard screening, with price 7.50 EUR.
Discount – a screening for children and students on a reduced price – 5.00 EUR.
Write a program that enters a type of screening (string), number of rows and number of columns in the hall (integer numbers) and calculates the total income from tickets from a full hall.
The result has to be printed in the same format as in the examples below – rounded up to 2 digits after the decimal point.

Input	    Output	
Premiere
10
12	      1440.00	

Normal
21
13	      2047.50
