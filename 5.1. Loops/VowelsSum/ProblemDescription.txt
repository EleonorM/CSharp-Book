09. Vowels Sum 
https://csharp-book.softuni.org/Content/Chapter-5-1-loops/examples-for-loops/example-vowels-sum/example-vowels-sum.html

Write a program that inputs text (string), calculates and prints the sum of the values of vowels according to the table below:

a 	e 	i 	o 	u
1 	2	  3	  4  	5

Input	      Output	
hello	      6

bamboo	    9

hi	        3

beer	      4
