05. Max Number 
https://csharp-book.softuni.org/Content/Chapter-5-1-loops/examples-for-loops/example-max-number/example-max-number.html

Write a program that enters n integers (n > 0) and finds the max number among them (the largest number). 
The first line of the input, specifies the number of integers n. 
The next n lines hold the integers, one per line. Examples:

Input	    Output
2
100
99	      100

3
-10
20
-30	      20

4
45
-20
7
99        99

1
999	      999

2
-1
-2	      -1
