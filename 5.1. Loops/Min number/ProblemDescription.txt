06. Min Number 
https://csharp-book.softuni.org/Content/Chapter-5-1-loops/examples-for-loops/example-min-number/example-min-number.html

Write a program that inputs n integers (n > 0) and finds the min number among them (the smallest number). 
First enter the number of integers n, then n numbers additionally, one per line.

Input	    Output
2
100
99	      99

3
-10
20
-30	      -30

4
45
-20
7
99        -20
